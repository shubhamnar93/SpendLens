# ECONOMICS.md

## Core Model

SpendLens is a lead-generation and qualification funnel for Credex (AI credit marketplace).  
The tool identifies users overspending on AI tools and directs them to purchase discounted AI credits via Credex. Revenue is generated when an audit user becomes a paying credit buyer.

This document estimates revenue per user, CAC by channel, required funnel conversion, and the path to $1M ARR in 18 months using realistic spreadsheet-style assumptions.

---

## 1. What is a converted lead worth?

Target users: developers, indie hackers, and small startups buying AI credits monthly.

Estimated average credit usage:
- Indie hackers: $50–150/month
- Small startups: $150–600/month
- Power users: $600–2000/month

Assume blended average spend = **$200/month**

Credex acts as a reseller marketplace.  
Typical margin for credit resellers = **25%**

Monthly revenue per user:
$200 × 25% = **$50/month**

Assume average retention = **12 months**

Lifetime value (LTV):
$50 × 12 = **$600 per converted user**

This is the anchor number for unit economics.

## 2. Customer acquisition cost (CAC) by channel

Monthly cost: $2,000 (founder time + tools)
Visitors: 1,500/month

Audit → purchase: 8% → 7 customers

CAC = $2,000 / 7 = **$285**

---

## 3. Profitability math

LTV = $600  
CAC = $235  

LTV:CAC ratio:
600 / 235 = **2.55**

This is already profitable. Small funnel improvements push this above the ideal 3× benchmark.

---

## 4. Required funnel conversion

Assume blended traffic cost ≈ $2 per visitor.

Visitors required per customer:
235 / 2 ≈ **118 visitors**

If visitor → audit = 5%:
118 visitors → 6 audits

To get 1 customer:
Audit → purchase must be **17%**

Required funnel:
- Visitor → Audit: 5%
- Audit → Credit purchase: **17%**
- Visitor → Customer ≈ 0.85%

This is realistic because audit users are high-intent buyers.

---

## 5. Path to $1M ARR in 18 months

Annual revenue per user:
$50 × 12 = **$600**

Customers needed for $1M ARR:
1,000,000 / 600 = **1,667 active customers**

Monthly customers required:
1,667 / 18 ≈ **93 customers per month**

---

### Traffic required

Visitor → Customer conversion ≈ 0.85%

Visitors needed monthly:
93 / 0.0085 ≈ **10,940 visitors/month**

≈ **11k monthly visitors**

This level of traffic is achievable via:
- SEO content library
- Community distribution
- Partnerships
- Viral sharing of audit reports

---

## 6. What must be true for success

To reach $1M ARR in 18 months:

1. Average buyer spends ≈ $200/month on AI credits  
2. Credex maintains ≥25% margin  
3. Audit completion ≥5%  
4. Audit → purchase ≥17%  
5. Traffic grows to ~11k/month within 12 months  

If these hold, SpendLens becomes a scalable acquisition engine for Credex.
